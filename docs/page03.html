Welcome To GVPCE!!

Gayatri Vidya Parishad College of Engineering (GVPCE) in Visakhapatnam is a renowned institution that has been contributing significantly to the field of technical education. Established with a commitment to excellence, the college is known for its academic rigor, state-of-the-art infrastructure, and a vibrant learning environment. GVPCE offers a wide range of undergraduate and postgraduate programs in engineering and technology, catering to the diverse interests and aspirations of students. The curriculum is designed to be industry-relevant, incorporating the latest advancements in technology and ensuring that students are well-equipped to meet the challenges of the rapidly evolving professional landscape.The college is committed to fostering a culture of inclusivity and diversity, creating an environment where every student feels welcome and respected. GVPCE actively promotes extracurricular activities, including cultural events, sports, and technical competitions, allowing students to explore their talents beyond the academic realm. GVPCE has established strong ties with industries and organizations, facilitating internships, placements, and collaborative research projects. The placement record of the college reflects the success of its graduates in securing rewarding careers in top companies.

One of the distinctive features of GVPCE is its experienced and dedicated faculty. The faculty members are not only experts in their respective fields but also mentors who guide and inspire students to achieve their full potential. The emphasis on research and innovation is evident through the various research centers and laboratories that provide students with hands-on experience and encourage a spirit of inquiry. The campus of GVPCE is spread across a sprawling area, providing a conducive environment for learning and personal growth. The infrastructure includes modern classrooms, well-equipped laboratories, a well-stocked library, and recreational facilities, ensuring a holistic development for the students.
